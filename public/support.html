Common questions

Why does SkyMap need my location?
Only to show where you are on the map and to track your progress along a route. It's optional — SkyMap still works fully for browsing and planning without it.

A skyway or business shows the wrong hours, or a route looks off.
Map data comes from OpenStreetMap, maintained by volunteers, so it's sometimes out of date. Use the Report an issue / Feedback link on the place or route in question, or email the address above.

Do I need an account?
No. SkyMap has no accounts, no sign-in, and no login of any kind.

Does SkyMap work offline?
Map data and app assets are cached after first use, so browsing already-visited areas works without a connection. Live location and fresh map tiles still need one.
